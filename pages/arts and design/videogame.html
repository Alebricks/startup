Agropip Defense

Agropip Defense is an educational game where you protect a maize plant from fall armyworms using Agropip creatures. At first, you manually crush the worms with your mouse, but as the game progresses, you'll need to invest in bioreactors to produce Agropip. This lets you create creatures that automatically attack the worms for you. Your goal is to stop the worms from reaching and destroying your plant.

All the game's art and music were created by our team. The instructions guide players on how to start by crushing worms and eventually producing Agropip to automate defense.

Agropip Defense is still in development, with new features and improvements planned for future updates. We are excited to continue enhancing the game to make it both more educational and enjoyable for players of all ages.

You can play it here: https://alebricks.itch.io/cogo-juego-prueba
