Business Model

Our project focuses on developing a highly effective, eco-friendly bio-pesticide derived from a unique active compound extracted from black pepper. Aimed at small and medium-sized agricultural producers, our solution provides a safe and sustainable alternative to conventional chemical pesticides, addressing both environmental and health concerns.

Unique Value Proposition

We offer a cost-effective, non-toxic bio-pesticide that not only combats pests but also enhances soil health and improves crop yield. With a focus on sustainability, our product supports organic and regenerative agricultural practices, ensuring minimal impact on the environment and beneficial organisms.

Problem

The fall armyworm is a severe threat to maize crops, causing significant losses in agricultural production. Most of the pesticides allowed in Mexico used to control this pest contain active compounds that not only have adverse effects on farmers' health but also pollute the environment and harm local biodiversity.

Existing Alternatives:

Conventional chemical pesticides, Systemic pesticides, Commercial biological pesticides, Integrated Pest Management (IPM), Genetically Modified Organisms (GMO) resistant to pests.

Solution

We offer a biopesticide from black pepper that naturally combats fall armyworm, acting as a repellent and disrupting pest development without harming the environment. It’s non-toxic, easy to apply, works quickly, reduces costs, and improves soil health.
